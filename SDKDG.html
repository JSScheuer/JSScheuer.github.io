Süddeutsches Kolloquium über Differentialgeometrie

After a few years break this traditional series was revived in Frankfurt (2024) and Vienna (2025). For convenience of those interested, I decided to set up this page to keep track about upcoming events. This is neither an official organisation page of any sort, nor am I an organiser. If you plan to hold this event at your institution, I would be happy about an email to avoid conflicts with other potential organisers.
